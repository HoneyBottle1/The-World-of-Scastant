The main cities of the Brantimek Empire are [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Brantimek Empire/Environment/Cities & Towns/City 1/Maps\|City 1]], [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Brantimek Empire/Environment/Cities & Towns/City 2/Overview\|City 2]], and [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Brantimek Empire/Environment/Cities & Towns/City 3/Overview\|City 3]].
From the few reports we've got of people sailing near the shores of the Brantimek Empire, it is a land of doom and despair. The south-eastern region is the home of many active volcanoes, which have consequently transformed how the land looks. It is always dark and hot, thick smog in the sky, along with cinders and ashes everyone on the floor and in the air. The land is very rocky, all greenery having been burnt or choked. rivers of molten magma flow instead of water, making the land slowly expand on the northern coast. The culture there is wildly different from that of the western world. Many fortresses were built along mountain ranges, and volcanoes are considered high places of worship.
