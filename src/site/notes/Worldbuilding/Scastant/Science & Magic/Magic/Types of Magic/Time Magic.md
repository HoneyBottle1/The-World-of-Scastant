Time Magic touches sequence, not destiny. It may hasten decay, delay an instant, or glimpse what has already been written, but it cannot undo consequence. Every use leaves the practitioner slightly out of rhythm with the world. 

Time Magic is the most difficult to master out of all, because of its instability, and more recently, because few scholars of this magic type still remain in the world, after the tragedy of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Valin Wastes/History#The Great Cataclysm\|Great Cataclysm]]. Time Magic is thus also a dangerous craft, able to wreak havoc on massive scales, if it was not for the Laws on Magic.
