The main cities of Azraine are [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Azraine/Environment/Cities & Towns/Kogtirm/History\|Kogtirm]], [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Azraine/Environment/Cities & Towns/Lubwiec/Overview\|Lubwiec]], and [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Azraine/Environment/Cities & Towns/Nar Lodir/Overview\|Nar Lodir]].
Azraine is a quite mountainous land, and is potentially the highest overall region in terms of elevation. It also used to be home to forests of tall trees. Its soils were very fertile and its mines plentiful in resources. But with the War in the East, a large portion of the population had to scrape all the resources of the Region to construct large fortresses in the mountains of the Great Line, to hold off the hordes of the undead. Thus, entire woodlands were cut down, mines opened quickly all over the land and ran dry almost as quickly, and most of the wildlife was hunted to near extinction. And when there were no more resources to be used for the fortresses, the people of Azraine turned to their own towns and cities, that they ransacked, pillaged and destroyed for any scraps of resources. The people were forced to abandon these cities which were now unlivable, and the remaining population either fled to the Tiondywarm Kingdom and beyond, or took refuge in the very fortresses their homes built. 
The fortresses themselves are wonders to behold. High up in the mountains or deep below the ground, the architects of Azraine were among they were wonders to behold. The architects of Azraine were renowned for their skills on the whole continent, and they did not choose modesty for their final homes. The deep halls of the fortresses have statues of the great fighters, the major fortresses span across multiple mountains, with large bridges high above the ground. These strongholds were, and still are to this day, the most impregnable battlments ever created in all of History.
