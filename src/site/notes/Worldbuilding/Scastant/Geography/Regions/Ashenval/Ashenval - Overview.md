Ashenval is a cold and desolate tundra. Its full area is quite unknown, as it expands north and west past the impassable blizzard lands. Few trees can be seen from the shores, although the land is quite bushy. There are also many hills, settlements usually residing in small valleys. The settlements themselves are usually made of wood, bone and animal skin, as the region has many wild animals roaming around, that the people of Ashenval like to hunt.
