# Overview
Alchemy is not magic in the vulgar sense, as it is not linked to the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/General Overview#The Concepts\|Earth]]. It alters nothing that was not already willing to change. Through heat, time, mixture, and separation, it transforms matter into tangible potions that have real abilities and uses. Those who call it inferior simply lack patience.
