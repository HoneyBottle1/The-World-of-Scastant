The main cities of the Homedons Northern Islands are [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/Environment/Cities & Towns/Virdenshølm/Overview\|Virdenshølm]], [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/Environment/Cities & Towns/Bjargvorðr/Overview\|Bjargvorðr]], and [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/Environment/Cities & Towns/Sævar/Overview\|Sævar]]. Smaller, but important towns and settlements include Éltjad, Veiðrvágr, and Steinnausa
The Northern Islands, and the north of Glifs Island, are characterised by tundras and taigas. The terrain is also quite sloped, with many hills and cliffs. The northernmost isles are covered in everlasting snows. The mountains and cliffs tend to form fjords, meeting the sea. These isles are also nestled in the cold waters of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Oceans, Seas, and bodies of water#Barrcaster Sea\|Barrcaster Sea]] and the edge of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Oceans, Seas, and bodies of water#Shoaling Ocean\|Shoaling Ocean]], where the cold climate creates many icebergs, detaching from the sea of ice at the northern edge of the world.
