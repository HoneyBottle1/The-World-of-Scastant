The main cities of the Everseen Council are the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Everseen Council/Environment/Cities & Towns/Capitol City/Overview\|Capitol City]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Everseen Council/Environment/Cities & Towns/Observatory City/Overview\|Observatory City]], and the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Everseen Council/Environment/Cities & Towns/Northern Village/Overview\|Northern Village]].
Most of the region is very lush and green, with a [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Forests#Musram Forest\|large forest]] to the south-west, and a [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Oceans, Seas, and bodies of water#Iris Reservoir\|major lake]]. There are some hills, though the region is mostly flat, allowing for watchtowers to spot potential invaders from very far away. To the south, the plains slowly transition into a small savanna band, before transitioning to the desert of [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Monaance Sultanate/Monaance Sultanate - Overview\|Monaance]]. To the north, beyond the mountains of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Mountain Chains#Silent Peaks\|Silent Peaks]], the land is colder, resembling that of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/Homedons Northern Islands - Overview\|Homedons Islands]], with a more sloped taiga.
