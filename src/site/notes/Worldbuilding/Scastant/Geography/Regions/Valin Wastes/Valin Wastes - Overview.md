The main cities of the Valin Wastes are [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Valin Wastes/Environment/Cities & Towns/Misegate/Overview\|Misegate]] and [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Valin Wastes/Environment/Cities & Towns/Necrosurb/Overview\|Necrosurb]].
Scholars and historians alike still debate on what the Valin Wastes looked like before the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Valin Wastes/History#The Great Cataclysm\|Great Cataclysm]]. Nowadays, the vast majority of the lands are covered in everlasting dunes of snow. Most of the cities and towns are now nothing but ruins, including the old capital Necrosurb. As reported by the few adventurers courageous enough to brave the harsh blizzard, there is not a single tree to be seen in the region, and the rivers and lakes are all frozen. No animal was ever seen roaming the white wastelands.
