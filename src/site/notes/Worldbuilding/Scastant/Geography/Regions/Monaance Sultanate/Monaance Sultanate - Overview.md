Monaance is the largest desert in the continent of Scastant. It is characterized by large sand dunes as far as the eye can sea, from the western coast of the Shoaling Ocean, to the Eastern coast of the Inner Ocean. The south-western part of the Region is most well-known for their towering cliffs of red sandstone, the Sandrial Cliffs. Small rivers originate from these cliffs and snake through the shifting sands of the Region, allowing for oases and general greenery to form along the banks of said rivers.
