The main city of the Gredan Sylvarsen is [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Gredan Sylvarsen/Environment/Cities & Towns/Nilalume/Overview\|Nilalume]], and many other [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Gredan Sylvarsen/Environment/Cities & Towns/Smaller Camps/Overview\|smaller camps]].
The Region of Gredan Sylvarsen is entirely covered in a large, luxuriant forest. Its trees are not found anywhere else in the world. Few outsiders have ever set foot in this forest, but they all report it is very dense and difficult to traverse with no guide. But they also say that at night, the forest transforms to become something else entirely. Magic itself flows through every single tree, bringing it to life. The trees, mushrooms and bushes start to glow with unnatural and ethereal colors, and the forest wildlife comes out to enjoy the light. These magnificent trees, still, pale in comparison to the mighty elegance of [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Gredan Sylvarsen/History#The Awakening of Gaanlion\|Gaanlion]], standing tall to the south of the peninsula, towering over all other trees. On clear days, its leaves can be seen from the other shore of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Oceans, Seas, and bodies of water#Sylvarsen\|Sylvarsen]].
