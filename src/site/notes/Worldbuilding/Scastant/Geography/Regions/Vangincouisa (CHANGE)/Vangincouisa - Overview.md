The main cities of Vangincouisa are [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Vangincouisa (CHANGE)/Environment/Cities & Towns/Pelguay Hideout/Overview\|Pelguay Hideout]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Vangincouisa (CHANGE)/Environment/Cities & Towns/Shefnan Grotto/Overview\|Shefnan Grotto]], and the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Vangincouisa (CHANGE)/Environment/Cities & Towns/The Restless Cavern/Overview\|Restless Cavern]].
Vangincouisa is a large savanna to the south of the world. From the feet of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Mountain Chains#The Great Line\|Great Line]] all the way to the impassable sand barrier to the south, where it becomes a treacherous desert. The land itself is quite flat, allowing people to see far to the horizon from any point in the Region. Recently, a lot of change has come to the Region, since the undead hordes of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Brantimek Empire/Brantimek Empire - Overview\|Brantimek Empire]] broke through the defenses to the east and invaded the Region. The few trees in the region have all been burnt, as has most of the greenery and wildlife.
