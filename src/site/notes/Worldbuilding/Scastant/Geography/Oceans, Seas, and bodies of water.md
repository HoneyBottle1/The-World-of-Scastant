# Oceans & Seas
### Barrcaster Sea
The Barrcaster Sea is the second largest sea on the continent of Scastant. It is a northern sea, often characterised with a dark blue as the colour of its water. The limits between the Barrcaster Sea and the Shoaling Ocean is determined by most people with the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/Homedons Northern Islands - Overview\|Northern Islands]]. 
It borders the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Weschamian Empire/Weschamian Empire - Overview\|Weschamian Empire]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Gredan Sylvarsen/Gredan Sylvarsen - Overview\|Gredan Sylvarsen]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Everseen Council/Everseen Council - Overview\|Everseen Council]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/Homedons Northern Islands - Overview\|Homedons Northern Islands]], and [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Glifs Island/Glifs Island - Overview\|Glifs Island]].
Area: ~ 231,198 km<sup>2</sup>
Average Depth: ~ 1,700 m
Deepest Point: ~ 3,870 m
### Shoaling Ocean
The Shoaling Ocean is the largest known ocean on the continental plateau of Scastant. Its water is of a beautiful sapphire blue, and it is said to be a relatively calm ocean. Therefore, trade developed quickly in the regions around the Shoaling Ocean. But this ocean is also very deep, joining with the never-ending oceans of the West that the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/History\|Homedonians]] ventured through many millennia ago.
It borders the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Homedons Northern Islands/Homedons Northern Islands - Overview\|Homedons Northern Islands]], [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Glifs Island/Glifs Island - Overview\|Glifs Island]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Everseen Council/Everseen Council - Overview\|Everseen Council]], and the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Monaance Sultanate/Monaance Sultanate - Overview\|Monaance Sultanate]].
Area: ~ 578,771 km<sup>2</sup>
Average Depth: ~ 4,950 m
Deepest Point: ~ 6,800 m
### Inner Ocean
The Inner Ocean, as its name conveys, is an inland sea. It is the second largest sea in Scastant. It is quite steep and deep, and it is rumoured to be the home of the Thalenvael, a species of highly intelligent and sentient sea creatures. Therefore, this ocean is also considered as its own [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Inner Ocean/Inner Ocean - Overview\|Region]], although its inhabitants are unknown to the population of the surface.
It borders the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Monaance Sultanate/Monaance Sultanate - Overview\|Monaance Sultanate]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Everseen Council/Everseen Council - Overview\|Everseen Council]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Weschamian Empire/Weschamian Empire - Overview\|Weschamian Empire]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Tiondywarm Kingdom/Tiondywarm Kingdom - Overview\|Tiondywarm Kingdom]], [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Azraine/Azraine - Overview\|Azraine]], and [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Vangincouisa (CHANGE)/Vangincouisa - Overview\|Vangincouisa]].

#### Derheim Bay 
Derheim Bay is located in the north-western part of the Inner Ocean. It borders the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Monaance Sultanate/Monaance Sultanate - Overview\|Sultanate of Monaance]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Everseen Council/Everseen Council - Overview\|Everseen Council]], and the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Weschamian Empire/Weschamian Empire - Overview\|Weschamian Empire]]. It is a major hub for trading and transportation. Pirates often sail across the bay to commandeer ships in the region. 
#### Oakrose Inlet
The Oakrose Inlet is a minor land formation to the south of the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Tiondywarm Kingdom/Tiondywarm Kingdom - Overview\|Tiondywarm Kingdom]]. It acts as a deep port site. 
### Black Expanse
The Black Expanse is the smallest sea on Scastant. It is among the roughest seas of the continent. Its waves crash harshly against the rocky cliffs on every sides. Because of the sea's weather, and the smokes and ashes coming from the volcanoes in the south-east, the waves look dark and hostile. That, and the black rocks, gave it its infamous name. 
It borders [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Ashenval/Ashenval - Overview\|Ashenval]], [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Duskenvar/Duskenvar - Overview\|Duskenvar]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Tiondywarm Kingdom/Tiondywarm Kingdom - Overview\|Tiondywarm Kingdom]], [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Azraine/Azraine - Overview\|Azraine]], and the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Brantimek Empire/Brantimek Empire - Overview\|Brantimek Empire]].

#### Sanguine Gulf
The Sanguine Gulf is located in the south of the Black Expanse. It borders [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Duskenvar/Duskenvar - Overview\|Duskenvar]], the [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Tiondywarm Kingdom/Tiondywarm Kingdom - Overview\|Tiondywarm Kingdom]], and [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Azraine/Azraine - Overview\|Azraine]]. Its geography allows for easy defence of its waters by the allied forces, against the occasional [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Brantimek Empire/Brantimek Empire - Overview\|Brantimek]] or [[Worldbuilding/Scastant/Geography/Regions/Ashenval/Ashenval - Overview\|Ashenval]] raids from the East. The coast is also lower there, allowing for easier construction of ships and ports.
